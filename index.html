How It Works

Learn about the process of e-waste recycling and how you can contribute to a cleaner planet.

Step 1: Collection

We collect old electronics from households and businesses.

Step 2: Sorting

We sort e-waste into categories based on material and type.

Step 3: Recycling

Electronics are recycled to recover valuable materials and reduce landfill waste.

Why Manage E-Waste?

  • Environmental Protection: Reduces hazardous waste and pollution.
  • Resource Recovery: Recovers valuable materials such as metals, plastics, and glass.
  • Energy Efficiency: Recycling e-waste uses less energy than producing new products.
